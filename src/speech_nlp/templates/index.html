📊 The Dataset

35 Trump rally speeches (2019–2020) chunked into ~2,354 semantic segments and indexed in ChromaDB as 768-dimensional MPNet embeddings. This is the knowledge base the RAG chatbot searches through. Hit the button to see what’s in there.
